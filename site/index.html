Built for people who live in more than one time zone.

Two at a glance

The clocks that matter most sit in the menu bar. Choose how many show — the rest are one hover away.

Flags, not guesswork

Each clock carries its country flag, so you read the right time without parsing a label.

Pick from the world

Search 60+ major cities by name, country, or zone. Drag to reorder. Add or remove anytime.

Stays out of the way

No Dock icon, no extra window. 12- or 24-hour, optional seconds, launch at login.

How it works

  1. Add your cities. Open the panel, search, and click to add. Drag to set the order.
  2. Set how many show. Two by default. The top clocks fill the menu bar.
  3. Hover for the rest. Move over the menu bar item to see every clock with date and offset.
